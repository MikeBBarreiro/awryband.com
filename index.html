A//WRY is a band started by two best friends from New Orleans (Lexie Wilson and Mike Barreiro). With influences from 90’s grunge to modern revival, their writing style is a new refreshing take on old styles. The band formed in late 2014 when vocalist Lexie Wilson, decided to ditch the solo act and reach for other members to help create a full sound. Mike Barreiro was the first she reached out to. Together they started creating YouTube covers just to test the waters as to what they could do together. The instant mesh was incredible. Within the first week of writing together, the two created their first single Express. The band is currently searching for the remaining members. Until then, check out their acoustic sets around Nashville for a look into what this new and coming band can do.
