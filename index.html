A//WRY is a band started by two best friends from New Orleans (Lexie Wilson and Mike Barreiro). With influences from 90’s grunge to modern revival, their writing style is a new refreshing take on old styles. The band formed in late 2014 when vocalist Lexie Wilson, decided to ditch the solo act and reach for other members to help create a full sound. Mike Barreiro was the first she reached out to. Together they started creating YouTube covers just to test the waters as to what they could do together. The instant mesh was incredible. Within the first week of writing together, the two created their first single Express. The band is currently searching for the remaining members. Until then, check out their acoustic sets around Nashville for a look into what this new and coming band can do.

Michael // Barreiro

Sup bitches! My name is Michael Barreiro, but my friends call me Mike. Music has always been a huge part of my life and I’m sure it has always been apart of yours. That being said, it’s never left my side. Every time I get a chance I’m playing drums, singing, writing weird riffs on a guitar, or just goofing off. I’ve been a screamer in my previous bands playing countless fun shows, but fate has led me to A//WRY as a guitarist. It all started in my apartment as small talk with lexie bringing up the conversation. It grew from there and here we are! we are growing and always writing new and powerful music. So keep in touch!

Lexie // Wilson

Hey hey! I’m Lexie Wilson and I am the vocalist of A//WRY. For a long time I committed myself to the solo act of singer songwriter, until one day the inspiration hit to take my music to the next level. I took the idea to Mike and it seemed to skyrocket really quickly. Music has always been a direct outlet for me ever since I got my first guitar on my tenth birthday. From then on the constant influence of my surroundings morphed into a sound that I couldn’t get out of my head. I hope that through A//WRY, we can give you a look into that sound, and create a new sound together. Keep a lookout for music and events in the near future. See you around!
